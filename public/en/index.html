What is Linux Session?

Linux Session is the biggest conference about Open Source solutions and latest trends in Linux-based systems

How does this work?

Entry is free. Every year enthusiasts and professionals visit us to learn and share their knowledge

Why?

We want to promote the idea of free software. We know that open source if getting more and more popular in commercial solutions and we want to promote it even more
